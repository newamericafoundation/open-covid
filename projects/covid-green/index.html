Covid Green

Repository https://github.com/covidgreen

Summary

COVID Green is an exposure notification app developed by the Government of Ireland and is compatible with the Google/Apple Exposure Notification (GAEN) API. It is hosted by Linux Foundation Public Health (LFPH), and has since been replicated in Scotland, Gibralter, Northern Ireland, Pennsylvania and Delaware.

Author Health Service Executive (HSE) of Ireland

Main point of contact info@lfph.io
