Pandemic EBT

Repository https://github.com/codeforamerica/pandemic-ebt
Live Site https://ca.p-ebt.org/en/

Summary

Rails app to support online Pandemic EBT (P-EBT) applications in California. P-EBT benefits help families in California buy food when school is closed. P-EBT has been replicated in Michigan.
